The Best Movies According to Jose

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

A spaghetti western with a dose of comedy, where two scammers join forces against a third to find buried gold.

The Godfather

An American mafia movie with an Italian immirgant family with ties to the mafia in Italy. The finest cast of all times.

Final Destination

A thriling horror franchise, with death using people as a vessel to haunt and kill the living.
